About Us

WE PROVIDE SECOND CHANCES TO REFUGEES THROUGH TECHNOLOGY.

Conflicts are erupting in several parts of Africa, including South Sudan and the Democratic Republic of Congo. This unfortunate reality is leading to a growing number of refugees seeking shelter, and the resources to accommodate them are becoming increasingly stretched thin.


With the region's limited resources, the few existing opportunities are fiercely competitive. As a result, many of these young individuals, brimming with untapped talent and creativity, are facing an uphill battle to secure even the most basic prospects.


A revolutionary program set to unleash the boundless potential and creativity of young refugees, and reshape their futures with endless possibilities! The project is implemented inside Kakuma refugee camp, which hosts nearly 300,000 refugees.

Our Mission

We connect, inspire, and empower young refugees to work together, lead the change, and create transformation.

Our Vision

We seek to promote an entrepreneurial mindset, connect people, and create an unstoppable stream of actions towards the transformation we want to see in the world.

Our work seeks to tackle digital and social exclusion by providing people with the skills and confidence they need to access digital technology
