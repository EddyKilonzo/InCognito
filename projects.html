Our Projects
Creative Hub

We provide free educational space that will be open for all visitors. Trained refugees themselves will be guiding people into the virtual worlds and helping them to make their VR creations. Added to that, there will be technology experts at the Hub both physically and virtually through telepresence.

Incubation

Our mission is to provide potential entrepreneurs with the tools and the support to execute sustainable change. Our most important goal is to empower a generation of problem-solvers in Africa

Peer Learning

This is a peer-to-peer mentoring program for female university students, made by women for women. The purpose of the program is to create supportive friendships, and to both inspire and be inspired by others. The program helps you gain cross-cultural communication skills and valuable insights about yourself.

Tech Hub

Our experienced tutors open new tech resources on AI ecosystem and expand refugees' access to the world through hands on training, one-on-one practicals and taking part in discussions. Participants Learn, get inspired, and network, and in the process build grounds for their professional career.

Ecosystem Mapping

We have networked with like-minded tech experts in the region to bring the best talent and resources available for participants

Acceleration

Our Online/offline program for refugees youth with interest in entrepreneurship, allowing founders to rapidly validate their problem, market, and solution.
